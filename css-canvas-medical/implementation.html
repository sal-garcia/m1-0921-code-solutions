Calculate an estimate of your ROI with Canvas

  1. Makign the Decision To Switch

    You should move forward with Canvas if and when you have determined that we far outperform other vendors and offer an unmatched return on investment. Our goals is to give you as much information as possible to make this determination one way or the other. We will provide access to a sandbox of the Canvas application for you to get a feel for the platform as well as references to current customers to talk about their experience.

  2. Interferances and Community Integration

    Canvas is meant to integrate with your existing interface and community resources. At a minimum, this means porting over your fax number, enrolling your payers with our clearinghouse, and getting bidirectional lab feeds running. We'll also load your preferred specialist and imaging centers into Canvas so that sending referrals and closing loops is seamless. Finally, if interfaces with health information exchange or other technology vendors are important, we will work with you to determine what we can deliver and when.

  3. Data Migration

    The first thing we will do is gain access to a full backup of your current data, which we have executed succesfully with both remotely hosted and on-premise EMR databases. Then our work begins to import into Canvas. Patient demographics and information like medications, problems, and allergies is relatively straightforward. While importing clinical notes and documents is more challenging, our team has been able to do this at a high level of fidelity with a wide variety of EMRs.

  4. Training and Site Visit

    We will work with you to configure Canvas properly, including clinical content, charge schedules, and much more. Role-based training will be done a few weeks prior to go-live to familiarize relevant team members with clinical, practice management, and billing workflows. Either before or during go-live, one of our team can visit your practice to work side-by-side with you on implementation and training.

  5. Go-Live and Tuning

    Go-live is when you start using Canvas with real patients in your practice. We will be with you, either in-person or remotely depending on your preference, to make sure everything goes smoothly. There is always fine tuning to do once you have a handful of encounters in Canvas under your belt: changing clinical microtemplates, setting up new automations, modifying practice management settings, and more. We are here to help with it all.

  6. Sustained Performance

    All EMR contracts should contain ssurances against broken promises and provide support over the long term. Our Performance Warranty means that if we are not meeting expectations, you can terminate the agreement and receive all paymnets back. (We do not expect this to happen). For our Standard plans, we will cover it. And for everyone, we provide unlimited 24/7 support at no charge. We expect this to be a long, productive partnership for us both.
